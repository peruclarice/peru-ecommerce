Background

regWand is a web platform that allows businesses to start, operate and grow their businesses within the African market by providing legal regulation data using a communicative AI.

Problem Brief

We found that on avg, it costs a startup entrepreneur $5,000 to consult a lawyer for regulatory advice to analyze their business and how to advance, googling for such answers on Google or Chat GPT may be conflicting and cause high risks in their businesses

Research

I initially needed to see if our hypothesis of this problem was valid. This included multiple google forms sent out to lawyers as they were our target audience After validating our hypothesis, we began with creating an empathy map to understand how best we can solve this problem The empathy map helped us define what our MVP would include

Research
Research Data Levaraged

By using statista, we found this opportunity that many startup entrepreneurs face, and by cutting down their legal fees in their startup capital, this opportunity was ready for grabs. By using an empathy map we discovered that startup entrepreneurs don’t have the time to stay on top of their business’ regulatory needs and would much rather have it done for them by hiring lawyers and focus on their main business objectives

Exploration/Solutions Considered

We birthed regWand, a comprehensive legal web app platform that specializes in providing regulatory information using communicative AI and also services to businesses, helping them start, operate and scale across various African markets.

Solution chosen and why

We found that this would help startup entrepreneurs focus mainly on their business’ objectives, since it would provide the following values:

  • Real time regulatory updates

  • Legal risks mitigated

  • Saving time & costs

Solution Validation

We tested our MVP through the following ways:

  • User Testing - We took the prototype through 2 tests with 2 different groups (startup entrepreneurs & lawyers)

  • A/B Testing - In the same 2 different groups, we provide two different versions of the web app prototype, to see which they’d gravitate more to as well as which helps them quickly onboard

Results After

We developed the prototype later into our first MVP and had up to 5 businesses onboarded and a revenue of up to $400

What I would have done differently

These are the lessons and learnings I got from working on this project

#1. Designing first before development

This was in the beginning point of my UI/UX design, so I immediately developed designs that I was thinking and not even considering confirming sketches with the client

#2. Prioritizing UI over UX

My biggest priority was to make things look pretty first over the functionality of the UI elements, this cost me a lot of valuable time when developing

#3. Evangelizing importance of UI/UX methodologies

Working in a small team, not much consideration was taken over the importance of UI/UX during the development process, which caused me to keep redoing designs over and over. I as both the designer and front-end developer should have spearheaded the importance of this more and perhaps what took us weeks to accomplish could have been cut in half

Background

A community of commuters were created to keep commuters engaged using a community on facebook https://www.facebook.com/trotrodiaries. With this growing community, the CEO saw it fit to create a website for the community to engage in with job postings, non-profit projects and many more

Problem Brief

Creating a website for an existing community that they could use to their advantage as well as grow their community in the same platform

My Accomplishments

Here are some of the accomplishments I have done and very proud of

Be Me Digital Inclusion Mentorship Program | 2024

I mentored highschool girls as a woman in STEM, I mentored on my profession as a UI/UX designer and frontend developer. The key purpose of this mentorship is to show young girls that they can pursue a profession in STEM and not be intimidated

Peru Designs Community | 2022 - Present

A design community where I mentor other potential and future junior designers on the principles of UI/UX design as well as figma design classes.

MEST Africa Community | 2022 - 2023

MEST incubator is a year long fully funded tech/business program where they take Africans from all walks of life, brings them into one room and help them come up with sustainable tech business startups, through the use of teaching coding, business and communication programs. Getting through the rigorous interview process of the MEST application and getting to be one of the 52 entrepreneurs in tech all across Africa is an achievement that has frankly changed my life. In terms of using my tech skills into functional businesses that would add value towards my society. I also got to advance my community and network on a global landscape.

Global Goals Model United Nation | 2019

I had the profound opportunity to join the GGMUN platform as a voluntary delegate where we debated on current world crisis with other delegates and youth leaders from across the globe and came to a consensus together. As a delegate in the platform I gained a lot of skills in Negotiation, Critical Thinking and especially Diplomacy

Nairobi Hackathon | 2018

This hackathon was carried out by the Nairobi Hackathon carried out at Strathmore University. During this competition my team of 5 girls called "Al-Galrithim" won the competition with over 200 participants. It was a competition overseed by industry experts such as Microsoft, Facebook and Angel Hack.

Girls Code Kenya | 2018

An NGO company that promotes tech development within the life of a girl. It promotes a technological culture and embraces the fact that a girl can do whatever she sets her mind on. Girls Code seeks the growth of technology especially in the sector of programming with major emphasis on web development. It holds holiday boot camps of 1 week to teach Highshool girls the basis of code. I contributed to the company as a teacher and a facilitated the smooth running of the boot camp operations.

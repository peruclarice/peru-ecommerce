Background

IPHero is a web application that provides with IP protection services to all form of African creatives, helping them earn more revenue from their creative works, protection of their online assets and give credit to them if a user uses their assets for their gain

Problem Brief

Creating a web app that could scan online for any IP that has been infringed upon by other users online and taking them down or giving credit back to the owner of the creative work

Creating a social platform, for the older generation, to find love and connections

Social Sector Figma

Background

A lot of older generation communities lack having a social life, especially since their children have grown up to explore their own lives, leaving them lacking and lonely, especially in the case if they’ve lost their loved ones/partners

Problem Brief

We wanted to see how best to solve the lonely epidemic that plagues the older generation by creating a social platform app for them

Solution chosen and why

    Why a mobile app:

  • Based on our research, the target normally interact with their phones more than laptops/desktops

  • It would be faster and more convenient to use and find connections and upcoming events

  • Having conversations that look similar to Whatsapp would help them, since they interact with Whatsapp daily, hence making the interaction with the app easier

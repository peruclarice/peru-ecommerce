Development

Solution chosen and why

Why a mobile app - a mobile app was the best solution as from our research we found that:

  • Small holder farmers normally use their mobile phones in their business dealings

  • Mobile money has become widely adopted in this market and was key

  • A Lot of our users don’t use smartphones, so we needed to provide a USSD version of the app in order to request/pay for their loans

What I would have done differently

During the duration of this project, the following were my findings:

#1. Importance of sketching and wireframing

The client provided me with creative liberty to designing the application, and I went straight to designing high-fidelity designs before wireframing them and sharing with the client

#2. Client payments

Because I worked solely as a freelance designer, client payments were not handled very smoothly, this became a headache, and learning how to navigate was a key learning point for me as a freelance designer
